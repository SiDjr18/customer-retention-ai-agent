What this agent can do

Universal File Upload

CSV, Excel (multi-sheet), PDF, and TXT. Large files up to 500 MB handled with chunked pandas reading.

🔍

7-Domain Inference

Automatically detects Customer Retention, Revenue Forecasting, HR Analytics, Credit Risk, Fraud Detection, Supply Chain, or General Analytics.

🤖

Multi-Agent Chat

5 specialist agents routed by keyword: Data Analyst, Retention Strategist, Scenario Planner, Executive Briefing, and Uploaded Data Agent.

What-if ROI calculator for retention budgets. Auto-adjusts risk thresholds when sigmoid-compressed scores yield empty cohorts.

Priority Customer List

Ranks customers by weighted score (50% churn risk + 30% CLV + 20% complaints). Adaptive percentile tiers — no hardcoded thresholds.

Report Generation

PDF, CSV, PowerPoint, and Markdown reports — all generated server-side using ReportLab and python-pptx, no cloud dependencies.
